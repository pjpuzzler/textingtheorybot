# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This app processes limited data needed to operate voting and annotation features.

## What data is processed
- Reddit user identifiers needed for vote tracking and anti-abuse checks.
- Vote selections (badge classifications and Elo votes).
- Post metadata and badge placement coordinates.
- Uploaded image media used to create posts.

## How data is used
- To render app functionality (create posts, collect votes, compute consensus).
- To apply anti-abuse controls (for example, eligibility checks and self-vote prevention).
- To update post/user flair where applicable.

## Data storage
- Operational app data is stored in Reddit/Devvit services (including Redis) as required for app behavior.
- Data is retained only as needed for app operation and moderation/safety purposes.

## Data sharing
- The app does not sell personal data.
- Data is not shared with third parties except as required by platform infrastructure or legal obligations.

## Your choices
- Do not use the app if you do not want your activity processed as described.
- You can remove your content/votes where platform features permit.

For questions about this policy, contact the app maintainer through the app listing or subreddit moderation channels.
