# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Texting Theory Bot processes to operate its screenshot annotation and voting features.

## 1. Data processed by the app

The app may process:

- Reddit account identifiers and usernames needed for vote tracking, anti-abuse checks, and moderation-related logic.
- Vote selections, including badge classifications and Elo votes.
- Post metadata, badge placement coordinates, consensus results, and related app state.
- Uploaded conversation screenshots and derived media used to create posts.
- Limited subreddit and account signals used to enforce integrity rules, such as account age, karma thresholds, and best-effort banned-user checks.

## 2. How data is used

The app uses this data to:

- Create and render posts and annotations.
- Store and update votes, consensus calculations, and flair behavior.
- Prevent self-voting, spam, abuse, vote manipulation, and other misuse.
- Support moderation, safety, debugging, and policy compliance.

## 3. Sensitive content and user responsibility

Because the app may process screenshots of private conversations, you should avoid uploading sensitive personal information unless you have a lawful basis and sufficient permission to do so.

You are responsible for reviewing screenshots before upload and removing or obscuring private information where appropriate.

## 4. Storage and retention

- Operational app data is stored using Reddit and Devvit infrastructure, including Reddit-hosted services such as Redis and media storage.
- Data is retained only as long as reasonably needed for app operation, integrity controls, moderation, safety, and related troubleshooting.
- Some data may persist in Reddit posts, comments, logs, backups, or platform systems outside direct app-level control.

## 5. Data sharing

- The app does not sell personal data.
- The app does not share data with independent third-party marketers or data brokers.
- Data may be processed by Reddit, Devvit platform infrastructure, service providers acting on Reddit's behalf, moderators acting within platform tools, or where required by law.

## 6. Your choices

- Do not use the app if you do not want your activity processed as described here.
- Content removal or other corrective action may be handled through Reddit platform tools or subreddit moderators.
- If you believe content shared through the app violates your rights or privacy, report it through Reddit tools and contact the app maintainer through the app listing or subreddit moderation channels.

## 7. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

## 8. Contact

For privacy questions or requests related to the app, contact the app maintainer through the app listing or the relevant subreddit moderation channels.
